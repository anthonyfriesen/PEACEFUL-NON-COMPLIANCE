What is Peaceful Non-Compliance?

Peaceful non-compliance is a form of protest and resistance rooted in non-violence. It is the deliberate choice to refuse participation in unjust systems, laws, or mandates, while maintaining compassion, respect, and peace. Inspired by figures like Gandhi, Martin Luther King Jr., and countless unsung heroes, this approach is a powerful tool for social change.

Core Principles

  • Non-Violence: Never respond to aggression with aggression.
  • Integrity: Align your actions with your values.
  • Community: Stand together and support each other.
  • Education: Know your rights and share knowledge.
  • Respect: Treat everyone—especially opponents—with dignity.
